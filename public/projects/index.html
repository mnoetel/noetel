PhD Thesis

My thesis looked at the effects of mindfulness in sport and performance settings. It included: a systematic review of studies that explore mindfulness in athletes; machine learning methods for making short questionnaires, with a mindfulness case study; and a double-blinded randomised experiment that tested a brief mindfulness induction in athletes Ultimately, I think the key lessons were that mindfulness isn’t the panacea that many make it out to be.
