I’m most interested in helping:

  • people with ambitious for-purpose projects that aim to change behaviour at scale
  • people trying to figure out what works to solve the world’s most pressing problems
  • people trying to synthesise complex secondary data sets
  • prospective students who want to do higher degrees by research (e.g, PhD)

I might be able to help you by:

  • translating top-level evidence into behaviour change using evidence-based multimedia design + engaging, effective training programs
  • conducting efficient, effective, and robust systematic reviews / overviews of reviews
  • running sophisticated meta-analytic techniques like network meta-analysis or meta-analytic structural equation models
  • being an agile, autonomy supportive leader and supervisor
